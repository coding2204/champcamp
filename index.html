About the Program

One week. The coaches who just won the tournaments you are trying to win.

What Champ Camp is

Champ Camp is a one-week online Lincoln-Douglas debate intensive running June 7–13. Built for competitive debaters who want to improve quickly — whether chasing first bids, trying to break at a national circuit tournament, or genuinely trying to understand the arguments at the top levels of the activity.

Every day runs from 11 AM to 4:30 PM CST. That is 5.5 hours of live instruction, practice, and feedback per day.

How the days are structured

Each coach runs their own block. You get instruction from specialists, not a generalist trying to cover everything. Our coaching staff covers the full range: policy and K strategy, philosophy and theory, practice rounds with live feedback.

Afternoons are built around practice rounds. Coaches will stop rounds, correct in the moment, and explain exactly what is happening and why.

Why we kept it small

There are camps with hundreds of students. This is not one of them. We capped enrollment so that every student gets real access — not a seat in a large lecture where you never speak directly with anyone.

Who runs it

Champ Camp was co-founded by Om Modi (Lynbrook HS, TOC Finalist, Glenbrooks Champion) and Atharva Srivastava (McNeil HS, TOC Semifinalist, TFA State Champion). Curriculum was developed by Bolang Zhu, TOC Champion.
